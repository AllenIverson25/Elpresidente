33rd President of the United States (1945-1953)

A decisive leader who shaped the post-World War II era and America's role in the world.

Early Life & Education

Born on May 8, 1884, in Lamar, Missouri, Harry S. Truman came from modest beginnings. The eldest of three children, he was born to John Anderson Truman and Martha Ellen Young Truman. Despite financial difficulties preventing him from completing college, Truman's determination and work ethic would later serve him well in his political career.
